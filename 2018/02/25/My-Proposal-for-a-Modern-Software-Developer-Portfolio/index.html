My Proposal for a Modern Software Developer Portfolio

Feb 25, 2018

“Intelligence is the ability to adapt to change.” - Stephen Hawking

Ten years ago (yes, I am that old 😃 ), my strongest point was a strong academic record. I got many offers just because of my rank in my class at the year of graduation, specially that the university I gratuadted from was one of the top 3 uninversties in Egypt at that time.

Now, I think it is different, not only in Egypt, but globally, have a look on 2018 Developer Skills Report by Hacker Rank, and before that, watch this very interesting talk by Malcolm Gladwell at Zeitgeist Americas 2013

So, if not for my academic record anymore, how I communicate my skills and abalities to employers or potential partners (in case of starting up a startup)? that’s what I will try to answer in the rest of the post.
